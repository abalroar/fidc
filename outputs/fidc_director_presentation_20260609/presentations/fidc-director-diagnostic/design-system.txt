Slide size: 1600x900.

Background:
- Main: warm paper (#F7F3EA).
- Cover / section emphasis: deep ink (#101820).
- Data panels: white or pale stone, no heavy rounded-card look.

Typography:
- Use Aptos/Arial-compatible sans for all text.
- Claim titles: 34-48px, bold, high contrast.
- Kicker: 12-14px all caps, tracked by spacing, centered with a small marker rule.
- Body: 16-22px.
- Labels: 11-14px.

Palette:
- Ink #101820 for text and dark backgrounds.
- Paper #F7F3EA and white #FFFFFF.
- Copper #C66A32 for primary volume marks.
- Teal #237B7B for CDI/spread dots and secondary proof.
- Blue #365D85 for document/cobertura proof.
- Green #557A46 for completed status.
- Red #9A3B3B only for caveats and partial downloads.

Chart grammar:
- Direct labels; avoid legends when labels fit.
- Volumes in R$ bi with one decimal.
- Percentages with one decimal.
- Bars encode volume/count; dots encode CDI+ or practice share.
- Dotted/secondary tracks indicate incomplete spread coverage.

Table grammar:
- Dark header, alternating paper/white rows, thin white separators.
- First column bold.
- No decorative table boxes beyond data grouping.

Footer/source:
- Quiet footer on every slide with source and page marker.

Banned motifs:
- No unverified logos.
- No stock imagery.
- No decorative blobs/orbs.
- No generic SaaS dashboard cards.
